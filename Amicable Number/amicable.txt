Subject: To Determine Amicable Numbers Pair.

284 = 1 x 284
    = 2 x 142
    = 4 x 71

Proper divisor of 284 = 1, 2, 4, 71, 142
1 + 2 + 4 + 71 + 142 = 220

Besides,
220 = 1 x 220
    = 2 x 110
    = 4 x 55
    = 5 x 44
    = 10 x 22
    = 11 x 20


Proper divisor of 220 = 1, 2, 4, 5, 10, 11, 20, 22, 44, 55, 110
1 + 2 + 4 + 5 + 10 + 11 + 20 + 22 + 44 + 55 + 110 = 284

So 220 & 284 is an Amicable pair.
